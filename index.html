About Me

I studied at The Art Institute of Washington, where i earned a bac+2 in graphic and web design. There I learned HTML, CSS, Javascript, Jquery, Illustrator, Indesign, Photoshop, etc. Originally I had planned to finish with a Diplome: bac+4. In the year 2021 I moved to France to learn the french language and continue my studies in web development and datat scsience. Adaptation, dedication, and learning new things are what motivates me. Changing my life for the sake of languages didn't come easily. Nothing that's worth it comes easy, and the reward of succeesing feels so much better.
